The Opportunity

Why Ireland, Why Now

Ireland is primed for a new wave of design-led, premium hospitality brands.

  • Growing demand for experiential, third-space concepts among urban professionals.
  • High spend per visit in coffee, all-day dining, and lifestyle-led spaces.
  • Under-penetration of global premium hospitality brands versus comparable European cities.

Brand Partners

For hospitality brands and franchisors

We act as your local partner for brand-led concepts entering Ireland, handling the detail required to launch and scale without diluting what makes the brand special.

  • Market mapping, site selection, and lease negotiation.
  • On-the-ground launch operations and local team build-out.
  • Protection of brand standards across design, service and experience.
  • Fewer, better locations with premium positioning over fast footprint expansion.
For concepts where design, ritual and consistency are non-negotiable, we translate your standard into the Irish market without compromise.

Capital Partners

For investors and capital partners

Corner House Ventures offers access to a curated pipeline of proven, international hospitality brands with clear unit economics and repeatable formats.

  • Asset-light structures with disciplined unit economics.
  • Exposure to high-loyalty, high-spend concepts.
  • Transparent performance reporting and alignment through co-investment.
  • Focus on concepts with defensible brand equity and multi-unit potential.
